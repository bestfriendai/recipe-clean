# RecipeClean Positioning

## Brand Identity

### Core Promise
**"Recipes without the noise"**

### Brand Personality
- **Tone**: Calm, helpful, uncluttered
- **Voice**: Direct, warm, human
- **Values**: Simplicity, privacy, speed

### Visual Identity
- **Primary Color**: Warm Coral (#FF6B5B)
- **Secondary**: Warm whites and grays
- **Style**: Minimal, content-first, Apple-inspired

## Positioning Statement

For home cooks who are frustrated by ad-heavy, feature-bloated recipe apps, RecipeClean is the minimalist recipe app that delivers just ingredients + steps—fast, offline, and without an account.

Unlike Paprika (expensive), Whisk (ad-heavy), and Copy Me That (complex), RecipeClean puts your recipes first with zero distractions.

## Unique Value Proposition

### Primary UVP
The only recipe app that respects your time, privacy, and attention.

### Supporting Benefits
1. **No ads** — Ever
2. **No account** — Start in seconds
3. **Offline** — Works in your kitchen
4. **Fast** — Opens instantly
5. **Simple** — Just recipes

## Target Segments

### Segment 1: The Frustrated User
- **Profile**: Tried other apps, annoyed by ads/complexity
- **Pain**: Time wasted, privacy concerns
- **Message**: "We heard you"

### Segment 2: The Minimalist Cook
- **Profile**: Knows what they like, wants no fuss
- **Pain**: Overwhelmed by features
- **Message**: "Less is more"

### Segment 3: The Privacy Conscious
- **Profile**: Doesn't trust app data practices
- **Pain**: Required accounts, data harvesting
- **Message**: "Your data stays yours"
