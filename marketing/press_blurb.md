# RecipeClean Press Release

## For Immediate Release

**RecipeClean Launches: The Minimalist Recipe App That Puts Cooking First**

**February 2026** — Today, RecipeClean announces the launch of its minimalist recipe management app for iOS and Android. Built for home cooks frustrated by ad-heavy, feature-bloated recipe apps, RecipeClean delivers just what users need: ingredients and steps—without the noise.

## Quote

> "We built RecipeClean because we're home cooks who are tired of every recipe app requiring an account, showing ads, and burying the recipe under a blog post. We just want ingredients and steps. RecipeClean is that simple."
> — RecipeClean Team

## About RecipeClean

RecipeClean is the minimalist recipe app that puts cooking first. With no ads, no account required, and offline functionality, RecipeClean respects users' time, privacy, and attention.

### Key Features:
- Add recipes from anywhere
- Powerful search
- Cook mode with check-off
- Works offline
- Privacy-first (no account)
- Premium: unlimited storage, photos, export

### Pricing:
- Free: Unlimited recipes, core features
- Premium: $4.99/month or $29.99/year
