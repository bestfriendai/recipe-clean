# RecipeClean - App Store Description

## Short Description
Simple recipe manager: save, search, and cook your favorite recipes. No ads, no account required.

## Full Description

**The simplest way to save and cook your favorite recipes.**

RecipeClean is the minimalist recipe app for home cooks who want ingredients and steps—without the blog stories, ads, or account requirements.

### Why Home Cooks Love RecipeClean:

• **Add recipes easily** — Type, paste, or capture recipes from anywhere
• **Powerful search** — Find recipes by name, ingredient, or description
• **Cook mode** — Check off ingredients and steps as you go
• **Save favorites** — Keep your go-to meals organized
• **Works offline** — Access your recipes anywhere, even without internet
• **No ads, no account** — Start cooking immediately

### Premium Features:
- Unlimited recipe storage
- Photo attachments
- Export recipes as PDF
- Cloud backup across devices

### Privacy First:
- No account required
- Your recipes stay on your device
- No data collection or tracking
